What is PMORGIS

PMORGIS, or 'Peoples Mart Online Receipt Generator and Inventory
System,' is a user-friendly web application built for People's Mart vendors.
It streamlines transaction processes, reduces errors, and saves time by
automating point-of-sale and inventory management, eliminating manual
paper-based record-keeping.

Features

With our features in PMORGIS, you can harness the full potential of your business. Explore the benefits of digital receipts, which eliminate the need for manual record-keeping. Take control of your inventory effectively with real-time updates, and enjoy a seamless user experience with our intuitive interface. Stay on top of your stock levels in real-time, preventing overstocking and streamlining your operations for maximum efficiency.

Digital Receipts

Vendors at People's Mart no longer need to manually write
down order details on the paper. They can input product
information like name, description, quantity, price, category
, etc. into our system, which instantly generates a digital
receipt for each order.

Inventory Management

Our system empowers People's Mart vendors to effortlessly
monitor their product inventory in real-time. They can add,
modify, delete, and update product information to simplify
inventory management.

Real-Time Inventory Tracking

Keep a close eye on the inventory levels in real-time, helping
vendors prevent both overstocking and understocking,
optimizing their stock levels for greater efficiency.

User-Friendly Interface

We offer an easy and intuitive interface.
